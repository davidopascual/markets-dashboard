TROUBLESHOOTING

Problem: "command not found: python3"
Solution: Make sure venv is activated
  source venv/bin/activate

Problem: "No module named 'yfinance'"
Solution: Venv may not be activated
  which python3  # Should show venv/bin/python3
  source venv/bin/activate

Problem: App takes too long to load
Solution: Check internet, first load is slower due to caching

Problem: No data displayed
Solution: Click "Refresh" button, check app.log for errors
